School Project
Custom Language Interpreter

An interpreter for the IPPcode2019 custom langeage which is a subset of a Three-Address Code.

It is made up of 2 separate script files:

  • A PHP (don't ask) parser that performs lexical analysis of the source code and converts the source code into a specific XML representation.
  • A Python interpreter that performs syntactic and semantic analysis of the XML representation and interprets it in the command line.

About Me

My name is Vojtěch Novotný. I'm 24 years old and I love computers, music and dance.

After graduating the 8-year high school Klvaňovo Gymnázium Kyjov with excelent grades, I decided to pursue computer science at the Faculty of Information Technology at the Brno University of Technology. But during my studies my interests shifted heavily and I temporarily lost interest in programing. I dropped out in third year and worked a variety of jobs, smaller and bigger including being a Dance Instructor.

Unfortunatelly COVID-19 put an end to that life. It made me realize what I want in my life and what I will have to do to achieve it. I decided to finally pursue a career in software development.

I still love dancing and music though. I would love to come back to teaching dance one day "after" the pandemic. I go social dancing as often as I can. I am even a part of a semiprofessional dance group. We perform in various venues and even plan to compete at the European Championship, if it doesn't get canceled. In what little time I have left I play my guitar and try to keep myself physically fit and healthy. You probably won't find me in the gym but you might run into me at a local bouldering crag with climbing shoes on and smudges of chalk all over.
