About Me

Hello, I am Vojtěch Novotný and I like games.

I like playing them and I love designing them.

Fortunatelly I live in Brno so hopefully I will get to make some soon.

I have been enjoying designing levels since I remember anything but parrents never saw games as something good so I never considered seriously doing it.

At school I was good at everything but for some reason I chose to study IT. I think I chose IT because of games but I honestly have no idea. As I was studying I kind of forgot about the idea that I could make games for a living. The programming has shrunk for me to about the size of one web application with a database. I was not enjoying the school and eventually dropped out. Only when I was about to drop out I kind of remembered why I went there in the first place. It was too late.

Now I have no job and no degree, but I have passion. Which sounds pathetic honestly, but maybe, just maybe I can make something off of it.
