About Me

Hello, I am Vojtěch Novotný and I like games.

I like playing them and I love designing them.

Fortunatelly I live in Brno so hopefully I will get to make some soon.

I love the though process that goes into it. Designing things for a user to have a perfect balance of understanding and challenge or exploration. To be immersive with their world and characters and story. To have fun mechanics that feel meaningful to the player and have depth. I love complicated systems ala Path of Exile and the world building of Hollow Knight, the PVP systems of World of Warcraft...

I bring this inspiration into my own creations, make something that is interesting to me and then present it to the player. Something new and different. (my blog)

Although I have spent a lot of my free time exploring game design but I never considered it a viable career. It wasn't even on my mind when it comes to my future jobs. It took me failing school due to lack of motivation and interest to realize games were the reason I chose the school in the first place. By then though it was of course too late.

Now I have no job and no degree. Bright future ahead...

But hey, at least I can dance...

Dance

In high school I discovered dancing and after a few years I finally landed in the swing dance family. Since then it has become my religion. I love the social aspects of it, the specific social setting and expectations. The dance itself is often exciting, sometimes thrilling, sometimes chill, laid back. Perfect way to spend the evening with some friends or meet some new people, listen to some great music.

It's a great incentive to travell too.

Right before the covid-19 crisis started I had the oportunity to start teaching Lindy Hop for one of the Brno based schools. Unfortunatelly 2020 had some special plans for everyone and I don't know when I'll get back to it.
